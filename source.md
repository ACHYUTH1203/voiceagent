# Lead Qualification Framework

The Voice Agent should ask:

1. What industry does your business operate in?
2. How many calls do you receive monthly?
3. What CRM are you currently using?
4. What challenges are you facing today?
5. Do you currently have a support or sales team?
6. What outcomes are you hoping to achieve?

Qualified Lead Indicators:

- High call volume
- Manual follow-up process
- Existing CRM
- Need for automation
- Customer support bottlenecks
